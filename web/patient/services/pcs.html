Pediatric Clinic

When your child needs to be hospitalized it is reassuring to know that at Emirates Hospital we offer a wide range of pediatric services with sensitive loving care in a warm and friendly environment. 

We are pleased you have chosen our Pediatric Service to meet the health needs of your child. Our Pediatric Service includes well child exams, immunizations, sports physicals, and treatment of acute illnesses and minor injuries. Your child’s health care will be managed by our team consisting of Family Doctors, Family Nurse Practitioners, and a Pediatrician. We serve a culturally diverse population, and provide timely medical advice and educational services. 

We recognize our responsibility for safeguarding the privacy of your health information. 


Friendly, Accessible Care  When a child is ill, Emirates physicians are available to do a careful examination, answer questions, and let parents know how we can help their child. Our doctors care for children with fevers, pain, ear and throat infections, rashes, colds and flu. 

We also help families find the best care and prevention techniques when children have chronic, ongoing problems, such as asthma (breathing problems), diabetes (high blood sugar), or allergies. 

WellChild Care
At Emirates Pediatric Clinic, we provide well-baby and well-child checkups. We also offer immunizations, school and sports examinations. 


24-Hour Medical Care
Parents know that if their children are seriously ill, they will have 24-hour care from a fully-trained pediatrician. 

Expert Multi-Lingual Staff
Our staff and physicians are multi-lingual, and care about the children of our diverse community. Our physicians and staff speak English, Arabic, Tagalog, French and Urdu. We are respectful of our patients and the rich cultures of our community.

 

Emirates Pediatric Center pediatricians are all Board Certified or Board Eligible physicians. 

There are extensive in-house lab and X-ray services 

Most major insurances are accepted. MasterCard, Visa & American Express are accepted. 

With selected office hours available every day, the PEDIATRIC CLINIC, P.A. strives to be responsive to your child's health care needs. An "on call" pediatrician is accessible 24 hours a day by calling (04) 3496666 and telling the hospital operator your need. 

The Medical Center Family Pharmacy hours coordinate with the Pediatric Clinic, allowing timely 7-day prescription service even during night clinic and week-end clinic hours.
